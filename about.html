About anavoroom

Who we are, what we believe, and why we exist.

Our Story

Born Out of a Passion for God's Presence

anavoroom was born out of a simple but powerful conviction: that when God's people gather with purpose, something extraordinary happens. We are not a building — we are a community.

What started as a vision to create space for people to encounter God has grown into a movement of worship, prayer, and community events that draw people together from across the region.

We believe the Church is never more powerful than when it is gathered and unified. Every event we host is a statement of that belief — that the presence of God is the greatest thing we can offer any room.

✨

Encounter

We don't want to put on events — we want to create encounters. Every gathering is designed to bring people into the presence of God.

🤝

Unity

We believe in the power of diverse communities coming together under one name. Walls come down when we gather as one.

🎵

Worship

Praise and worship are not warm-ups — they are the main event. Anointed music is at the heart of everything we do.

🌍

Service

We are called to give, serve, and love beyond our walls. As a registered charity, serving our community is central to who we are.

Vision & Mission

Why We Exist

Our Vision

To create spaces where every person — regardless of background — can encounter the love and power of God through community, worship, and the Word.

Our Mission

We host large-scale Christian events, cultivate authentic community, and serve those around us with the love of Christ — because we believe the world changes when God shows up.

What We Believe

Our Faith

📖

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God — our foundation for faith and life.

✝️

Salvation

We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ — his death, resurrection, and lordship over all.

🕊️

The Holy Spirit

We are a Spirit-filled community. We believe in the ongoing gifts and power of the Holy Spirit for today.

Prayer

Prayer is our lifeline. We believe in the power of corporate and personal prayer to move mountains.
