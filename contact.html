FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about anavoroom.

What kind of events does anavoroom organise?

We host large-scale Christian events including worship nights, prayer gatherings, and community conferences. Every event is designed to create space for people to encounter the living God.

How do I find out the venue address?

We share venue details directly — just message us on Instagram (@anavoroom), email anavoroom@gmail.com, or fill in the contact form on this page and we'll send you everything you need.

Is there a cost to attend?

Details about tickets or admission will be shared via our social media and Linktree closer to each event. Follow us on Instagram and subscribe on YouTube to stay updated.

How can I support anavoroom financially?

We are a registered charity and welcome donations via bank transfer. See the "Give" section above for account details. You can also contact us to discuss other ways to support the mission.

Can I volunteer or get involved?

Absolutely — we'd love to have you. Send us an email or fill in the contact form letting us know how you'd like to serve and we'll be in touch.

Do I need to be a Christian to come?

Not at all. Everyone is welcome at an anavoroom event, regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey. Come as you are — there's room for you here.
