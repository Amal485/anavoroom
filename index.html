Who We Are

A Community Built Around Encounter

anavoroom is a Spirit-led Christian community passionate about hosting large-scale events where people can encounter the living God. We believe in the power of gathering — for worship, prayer, and real community.

From worship nights to conferences, every event we create is an invitation to experience something greater. We are not just putting on shows — we are creating moments that change lives.
