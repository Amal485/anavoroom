Who We Are

A Community Built Around Encounter

anavoroom is a Spirit-led Christian community passionate about hosting large-scale events where people encounter the living God. Every event is an invitation to experience something greater.

We are a registered UK charity — no fees go to booking platforms. When you buy a ticket directly through us, every penny goes to making the event happen.
